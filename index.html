Welcome to MilkyWay Hotel & Resort

Located at San Juan, Baguio City. Our Hotel is a mixture of sophisticated and relaxed ambience offering a big range of quality services and a modern design & decoration, It is one of the most modern four star hotels in the city with 100 luxury guests rooms, the location is close in the city centre make the access to the historical parts of town easy by car or walking down the beautiful city of Baguio.


Take a walk to nearby Session Road and discover unique restaurants. Buy farm-fresh vegetables and fruits, as well as local delicacies and products at the Baguio City Public Market. Explore tourist destinations old and new such as Burnham Park, Camp John Hay, and BenCab Museum. For more fun and adventure, you can also go strawberry picking at La Trinidad Strawberry Farm.
